Welcome aboard!

2018/05/29

Protégés, welcome to what might be one of the most important summer experiences of your professional and academic life! We have a wonderful summer planned for you in the summer writing and computation workshops, so hang on — it is going to be an amazing ride.

Hopefully, you had some time over the weekend to think about what you would like to accomplish this summer and are ready to leap into action. Your research will keep you engaged and more than busy over the next 10 weeks, but I’m hoping the CTDS workshops provide a healthy supplement to your day-to-day, while also giving you some new ideas, tools and inspiration. We’re going to have a lot of fun and I am looking forward sharing and learning with you in through this journey.

During the summer, these workshops are yours and I would very much like you to be my partner in shaping the content towards your needs. Please feel free to reach out to me at anytime (before, during or after) to provide me suggestions on what you’d like to explore. Don’t be shy … I’m just an email, hangout or chat away when we’re not in a workshop, and soon I’ll post office hours if you’d just like to pop in for a little one-on-one.

Let’s make this an amazing summer together!

Keith
